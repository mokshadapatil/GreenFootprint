GREEN FOOTPRINT

Understanding your carbon footprint is crucial for several reasons. In India, the average carbon footprint per person is approximately 1.73 metric tons, significantly lower than the global average of around 4 metric tons. This knowledge empowers individuals to take personal responsibility for their environmental impact and encourages sustainable living choices. Reducing your carbon footprint can lead to cost savings through energy-efficient practices, potentially lowering utility bills and fuel expenses. Additionally, it brings health benefits by improving air quality and reducing pollutants, which can decrease respiratory diseases and other health issues. Ultimately, by knowing and reducing your carbon footprint, you contribute to global efforts to combat climate change, ensuring a healthier planet for future generations.
